About mountain

A mountain is an elevated portion of the surface of a planet, generally with steep sides that show significant exposed bedrock. Although definitions vary, a mountain may differ from a plateau in having a limited summit area, and is usually higher than a hill, typically rising at least several hundred metres a few thousand feet above the surrounding land. A few mountains are isolated summits, but most occur in mountain ranges.
