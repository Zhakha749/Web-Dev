Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, milk, and egg in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan and pour some batter.

Step 3: Cook both sides until golden.

Recipe #2: Homemade Pizza

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 base
Tomato sauce ½ cup
Cheese 200 g
Toppings As desired

Step 1: Spread sauce over the dough.

Step 2: Add cheese and toppings.

Step 3: Bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.
